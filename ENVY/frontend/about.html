About ENVY

ENVY was born from a simple observation: crypto traders deserve better tools. While the market never sleeps, most trading journals feel like they were built a decade ago—clunky, slow, and disconnected from real-time data.

We built ENVY to change that. A trading journal that respects your intelligence and your time. Real-time price feeds via WebSocket. Independent timers for every trade. Mathematical planning tools that help you calculate risk before you commit capital.

"The goal isn't to be the biggest trading journal. It's to be the one that serious traders actually want to use."

ENVY is independent and self-funded. We don't sell your data, we don't run ads, and we don't take venture capital that would pressure us to compromise on privacy or user experience.

This is version 1.0—the foundation. We're actively developing new features based on feedback from real traders.

— The ENVY Team

Built for traders, by traders.
