How ENVY Works

Three simple steps to professional trade journaling

1

Connect & Track

Create your free account in seconds. ENVY connects directly to Bybit's live market data via WebSocket, giving you real-time prices for over 450 cryptocurrencies. Select your favorite assets and watch your portfolio come alive.

2

Journal Every Trade

Log your spot and futures trades with precision. Enter your entry price, amount, and fees. Each trade gets its own independent timer that runs until you close the position. Monitor real-time P&L as the market moves.

3

Analyze & Improve

Review your performance with comprehensive analytics. Track win rate, profit factor, and trade duration. Use the mathematical planner to calculate optimal entries before your next trade. Master your strategy through data.
